## 🚀 Growth Hacks

### Twitter Growth

1. **Reply to big accounts** in your niche (early, valuable comments)
2. **Use trending hashtags** (#buildinpublic, #SaaS, #indiehackers)
3. **Post at optimal times** (9 AM, 1 PM, 6 PM PST)
4. **Thread format** (8-12 tweets, strong hook)
5. **Visual content** (GIFs, screenshots get 2x engagement)

### LinkedIn Growth

1. **Post long-form** (1,000+ characters perform better)
2. **Use line breaks** (white space increases readability)
3. **Tag relevant people** (but don't overdo it)
4. **Engage in comments** (first 2 hours critical)
5. **Post Tuesday-Thursday** (best engagement)

### Reddit Growth

1. **Be authentic** (no marketing speak)
2. **Provide value first** (answer questions, help others)
3. **Post in multiple subs** (but customize for each)
4. **Engage in comments** (respond to everyone)
5. **Share real metrics** (transparency wins)

## 📱 Platform-Specific Tips

### Twitter/X

- **Character limit:** 280 per tweet (threads for longer)
- **Best times:** 9 AM, 1 PM, 6 PM PST
- **Frequency:** 3-5 tweets/day
- **Hashtags:** 2-3 max (#buildinpublic, #SaaS)
- **Visuals:** GIFs, screenshots, memes

### LinkedIn

- **Character limit:** 3,000 (but 1,000-1,500 is sweet spot)
- **Best times:** 8-10 AM, 12 PM, 5-6 PM PST
- **Frequency:** 1 post/day
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 (#SaaS, #IndieHackers, #Startup)
- **Visuals:** Professional images, charts, headshots

### Reddit

- **Title limit:** 300 characters (but <100 is better)
- **Best times:** 6-9 AM PST (catch East Coast + Europe)
- **Frequency:** 1 post/subreddit/week (don't spam)
- **Format:** Text posts get more engagement than links
- **Engagement:** Respond to EVERY comment
